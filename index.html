MY 4 MAIN IDEAS



1. Reduced food waste policies should be encouraged from the production stage until food
is eaten or performance is achieved this is so that whenever possible, the amount of wasted food is reduced



2. The transport and warehousing facilities have to be improved as much as
possible to minimize any food wastage, and ensure that food reaches the correct people.



3. Advancement of education and other resources needed by farmers seeking to understand new
ways of farming and the need to increase their focus on crops as well as the need for environmental conservation.



4. Education for nutrition and health to improve food intake and
maintain the ideas of good health through a good diet
